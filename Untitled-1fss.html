About Red Barrels

Red Barrels is the Montreal-based independent studio behind the immersive horror franchise Outlast, a series that has terrified more than 37 million players worldwide. Red Barrels has made it their mission to create unforgettable experiences that leave players’ blood running cold.

A thrilling survival horror multiplayer take on the Outlast franchise.

The Outlast Trials

It’s 1959, and the trans-national Murkoff Corporation is using a recruitment agency to identify potential test subjects to turn into the perfect sleeper agents. These “Reagents” are forced to undergo terrible physical and psychological trials, until they have been conditioned for perfect obedience, ready to be released back into society, waiting to be triggered at just the opportune time

Outlast ||

You are Blake Langermann, an investigative journalist following a trail of clues that started with the seemingly impossible murder of a pregnant woman known only as Jane Doe. The investigation has lead you miles into the Arizona desert, to a darkness so deep that no one could shed light upon it, and a corruption so profound that going mad may be the only sane thing to do.
